Refund Policy

Example Public School

Our Refund Guidelines

Thank you for choosing Example Public School. We value transparency and want to ensure parents and guardians clearly understand our refund policy regarding admissions, fees, and related charges.

1. Admission / Registration Fee

The admission or registration fee is non-refundable under any circumstances once payment is made and the student's seat is reserved.

2. Tuition Fees

Tuition fees paid for a term, month, or year are non-refundable after the academic cycle has begun. A refund may be considered only if the student withdraws before the first day of classes.

3. Duplicate Payments

If a parent accidentally makes a duplicate or excess payment, the school will verify the transaction and issue a full refund within 7–10 working days.

4. Documents or Uniform Fees

Fees paid for uniforms, books, ID cards, certificates, or other physical items are non-refundable once purchased or collected.

5. Event or Activity Fees

Fees paid for tours, competitions, or special school events will be refunded only if the school cancels the program.

6. Refund Processing Time

All approved refunds (if any) will be processed within 7–14 working days, either via bank transfer or the original payment method.

7. How to Request a Refund

Parents can submit a refund request at the school office or email us at exampleschool@gmail.com with:

  • Student Name
  • Class & Section
  • Payment receipt or screenshot
  • Reason for refund request

The school management reserves the right to approve or reject any refund request based on policy.
